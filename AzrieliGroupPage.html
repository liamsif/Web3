Group Membership

The Azrieli Group has (as of 2020) five main arms:

Buying Peace Investments Ltd. Azrieli E-Commerce Ltd. Otzem Entrepreneurship and Investments Ltd. International Consultants Ltd. Azrieli Data centers LLC: An American subsidiary that deals with data centers, invests 23.95% in Compass, which deals in this field. In addition, direct holdings in properties abroad, at Lehavim Palace and Modiin Sheltered Housing Ltd. and 3.1% from Bank Leumi.

Azrieli Sharona, a mall and office building owned by the group, the tallest building in the country Company Holdings The company specializes in initiating, planning the construction and management of real estate projects in general and malls in particular, currently (2021) the group owns 18 malls in Israel from Acre to Beer Sheva, 13 office properties and 5 sheltered housing, as well as 8 office properties outside Israel.
